## MSc, BSc and PUK student projects

We provide a large range of different student projects within our [research topics](/research). We do not actively maintain a catalog of project suggestions, but encourage students to contact us to help design a project that fits both interest and background.

### Recent examples of projects

- Astrid Mørkholt Toft (MSc, 2026): Dynamical response of the Greenland Ice Sheet to climate change
- Jakob Skyggebjerg Kjær (MSc, 2025): Ice sheet model emulation and projection in response to climate change
- Josephine Gondán Kande (MSc, 2025): Predicting Greenland Ice Sheet Mass Balance - Exploring Training Strategies for Machine Learning
- Theo Häußler (MSc, 2025): Modeling Crystal Orientation Fabrics in Ice Cores and Ice - Streams: A Focus on Homogenization
- Isabel Schwermer (MSc, 2025): Modelling the evolution of the Greenland ice sheet over glacial-interglacial cycles
- Athene Elizabeth Stuart Demuth (MSc, 2025): Modeling the evolution of crystal orientation fabrics in an Antarctic ice shelf
- Chenan Di (MSc, 2025): Initialization of the Greenland Ice Sheet Using the Parallel Ice Sheet Model
- Luisa Elisabeth Hirche (MSc, 2025): Evolution of Olivine Crystal Fabrics in Earth’s Upper Mantle
- Majbritt Kristin Eckert (MSc, 2024): Modelling the Greenland Ice Sheet in Past Warmer Climates - Investigating the Impact of Domain Choice and Oceanic Forcing on the Last Two Deglaciations
- André Emil Toft Jensen (MSc, 2024): Remote sensing of Arctic surface albedo feedback
- Laura Lund Rysager (MSc, 2024): The Evolution of Crystal Orientation Fabric and Ice Flow in Ice Streams
- Yannick Elias Heiser (MSc, 2023):  The role of grain size on ice properties and deformation - Assessing new grain size data from the NEEM ice core generated by microstructure mapping.
